@FOOODIESADVENTURES

I have always been an avid foodie and started a "foodstagram" in high school. At first, my followers just consisted of friends and family, but I started to use relevant hashtags to attract a bigger audience. Three years later, my foodsta is run by myself in Boston, and by my friend Claire in New York. We have a much larger following and we have helped multiple restaurants in the Boston and New York area promote their foods.

Our photos are often re-posted by other foodies which is featured under the #fooodiesadventures hashtag.
