ALL THINGS LUNA

Get to know more about me!

Hi! My name is Luna Zhang, and I am from Newton, Massachusetts. I am a first generation college student at Babson College, concentrating in Operations Management and Information Technology Management. I am passionate about global supply chain, product management, and problem solving through design thinking, and hope to find a future in the creative part of the business world. As an organized and results driven worker, I thrive in fast-paced environments and aim to pursue challenges outside of my comfort zone.

WHERE AM I CURRENTLY

This summer, I am interning for Bigbelly Solar, a waste management company that provides smart and sustainable solutions for cities. The company was founded at my college as well as Olin College of Engineering. I was drawn to the company's initiative to reduce energy usage and increase efficiency through their solar powered trash cans. I work closely with the Finance and Operations team to execute numerous tasks such as: managing the Credit and Collections process in order to help the company lower their Receivables, fulfilling shipment orders on Netsuite, and creating and customizing Salesforce reports. Working in a startup environment has been such a rewarding experience-the company is a tight knit community and I have learned a lot from my co-workers.


In addition to Bigbelly, you can find me at Starbucks making drinks every Sunday. I have been a barista at Starbucks since I was in high school. Although it may not seem like it, being a barista has been one of the toughest jobs I have ever worked. I am constantly on my feet making drinks, stocking items, and making sure that customers are satisfied. The job requires a lot of multitasking and being two steps ahead at all times. The biggest skill I have gained from being a barista is learning how to work in a team setting-each barista has their own job, and if one barista is struggling, we all do what we can to support them so that our store is performing at its best. My coworkers are some of the most genuine and interesting people I have ever met, and seeing my favorite customers always makes my day.

MY CAREER INTERESTS

As an incoming junior in college, I am still exploring different career paths through taking various courses. Currently, I am pursuing a career in product management and operations management with hopes of landing a career that will allow me to explore my interests . My eventual goal is to be a product manager-a role where I am able to participate in design process but also manage the operations and brand aspect of the product as well. I am also interested in finding ways to make current supply chains more sustainable in the future, and would love to work with companies that are bringing supply chain management to the next level.
