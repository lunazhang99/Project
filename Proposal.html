Website Proposal

What is my website about>

I want to make my website a personal portfolio that contains fashion and digital designs that I have made. I currently have a personal website through Adobe Portfolio which I am paying for so I want to be able to make it myself too, so that I can add my personal aesthetic to the website.

Why is this site important?

This website is important because I can showcase my work to future employers. I want to hopefully get into the design industry, so having a portfolio is vital.

Target Audience

My target audience is employers, friends and family, and also people who are interested in my work.

Potential Content

I will be posting my fashion designs, digital designs, social media branding, events I have participated in, and an About Me page
