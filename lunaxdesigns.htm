LUNA X DESIGNS

Welcome to my passion project, LUNA x DESIGNS! Ever since I was in high school, I loved going to thrifting to find vintage gems. On weekends, my friends and I would always head over to Garment District's "pay-per pound" where clothes were sometimes only a dollar a pound! Finding a good piece was like looking for a needle in a haystack, but once you found the needle, it felt like hitting the jackpot.


Like every normal human being, I eventually got tired of certain styles, and resorted to either re-selling my clothes or donating them. One night, I was listening to the Rolling Stones, and thought to myself "I wish I owned a Rolling Stones shirt!". And then I said, "why can't I just make one myself?". I grabbed a few acrylic paints, an old denim jacket, and got to work. Next thing I knew, LUNA x DESIGNS was born.


My hobby of re-purposing clothes eventually led me towards a greater purpose that I want to pursue in my life. I hope to find a career where I am integrating sustainable practices into supply chains or products lines. LUNA x DESIGNS is just the start of this journey, and I am excited to see where I end up next.
